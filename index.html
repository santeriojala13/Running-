Paavo Nurmi Marathon 2025

I participated in the Paavo Nurmi Marathon 2025 in Turku, Finland. The atmosphere was incredible – spectators lined the streets, cheering every step. This year I managed to improve my time by several minutes compared to 2024, and the finish line sprint was one of my proudest moments as a runner.

Paavo Nurmi Marathon 2024

My earlier Paavo Nurmi Marathon experience in 2024 taught me a lot about pacing and mental strength. I struggled in the second half, but crossing the finish line was still a huge achievement. These lessons shaped how I prepared for 2025.
